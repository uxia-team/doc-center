Uxia helps teams validate digital experiences faster, without losing the depth of real UX insight.

Instead of spending days or weeks recruiting participants, scheduling sessions, and preparing reports, you can define your target audience, launch a test, and receive a detailed UX report in minutes.

Uxia uses AI synthetic testers to interact with your product, complete tasks, think aloud, and identify friction points across the experience. This helps you understand what users may struggle with, why it happens, and what to improve next.

You can use Uxia to test prototypes, static designs, Figma flows, live websites, or existing products. You can also combine AI-generated insights with feedback from real human participants when you need an extra layer of validation.

## Why teams choose Uxia

### Speed

Traditional user testing can take days or weeks from recruitment to final report. Uxia helps you get results in minutes, so your team can test more often and make decisions faster.

### Cost

Recruiting participants, paying incentives, scheduling sessions, and using multiple research tools can quickly become expensive. Uxia’s credit-based model lets you pay based on what you run and start with a free trial.

### Depth

Uxia does more than generate a high-level summary. Each test includes synthetic tester profiles, task-by-task transcripts, friction points, UX issues, and recommendations so you can understand both what happened and why.

### Audience control

You can define the type of audience you want to test with, including demographic, behavioral, and contextual characteristics. This helps you evaluate the experience from the perspective of the users who matter most to your product.

### Hybrid research

Uxia is not limited to AI testing. You can also collect feedback from human participants and compare both sources of insight in one place.
