AI User Research lets you gather deep qualitative feedback by asking [synthetic testers](/audiences/overview) direct questions — about your product, your messaging, your concepts, or anything else you need to understand. Instead of navigating an interface, testers answer in their own words, giving you the _why_ behind user behavior at a speed and scale traditional research can't match.

<Note>
  AI User Research and AI User Tests are complementary, not interchangeable. An **AI User Test** observes how testers _navigate and interact_ with an interface — it's behavioral. **AI User Research** asks testers _what they think, feel, and prefer_ — it's attitudinal. Use research to validate ideas and understand opinions; use testing to find UX friction.
</Note>

## What you can ask

Build a study from six [question types](/question-types), mixed and matched to get both quantitative signal and qualitative depth in a single run:

- **Open-ended** — testers describe experiences and explain their reasoning in their own words; best for uncovering motivations and unexpected insights
- **Rating Scale** — numeric scores (1–5, 1–7, or 1–10) to quantify sentiment and satisfaction
- **Multiple Choice** — present defined options, single- or multi-select, and see which resonate most
- **Yes / No** — a clear binary signal on a claim, feature, or value proposition
- **Ranking** — have testers order a set of items by preference or priority
- **Budget Allocation** — testers split 100 points across items to reveal where priorities really sit

## What you get

Responses are generated immediately — no recruiting, scheduling, or incentives. You get each tester's answers in their own words, plus a shareable [insights report](/responses) that synthesizes themes and standout quotes. And because it's synthetic, you can [chat with any tester](/chat-with-testers) afterward to follow up, probe for detail, or push back on a response — the flexibility of a one-on-one interview at the scale of a survey.

To ground responses in your real users rather than a generic population, build your audience with [Audience Enrichment](/audiences/enrichment): upload survey responses, interview notes, CRM attributes, or behavioral profiles, and Uxia constructs testers who think and respond like the people you're actually trying to reach.
