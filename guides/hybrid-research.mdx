AI testing gives you speed and consistency that traditional research can't match — results in minutes, zero scheduling, and no participant no-shows. Human testing gives you emotional authenticity, stakeholder confidence, and the kind of edge-case behavior that only real people produce. You don't have to choose. Uxia's hybrid research workflow lets you run AI tests first to find the signal fast, then bring in real participants to deepen and validate what you found — all inside the same workspace.

## Step-by-Step Hybrid Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Run your AI User Test">
    Create an AI User Test in Uxia, define your audience, write your mission, select testers, and launch. You'll have AI results — think-aloud transcripts, an insights report, and UX benchmark scores — in under 20 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review AI insights">
    Read the Insights Report and transcripts. Identify the top usability issues, confusion points, and areas of interest. At this stage, you're looking for patterns that either need human validation or that are clear enough to act on immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate a human test link from the same test">
    From your completed AI test, click **Create Human Test**. Uxia generates a shareable link tied to the same test setup — same mission, same prototype or URL. You don't need to rebuild anything.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share the link with your own participants">
    Send the link to your own users, customers, beta testers, or recruited panel. Participants open the link in any browser — no app to install, no account to create. When they're ready, they grant the necessary recording permissions and begin the test.

    <Note>
      Uxia does not currently provide or recruit human participants. You bring your own — existing customers, recruited users, internal stakeholders, or a panel from your own research operations.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Participants record their sessions">
    Each participant completes the test in their own browser. Uxia captures everything they do and say during the session. You get a full recording for each participant alongside their interaction data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review combined AI and human results side by side">
    Once human sessions are complete, all results live in the same Uxia test. Compare AI tester transcripts with human participant transcripts, look for where patterns align and where they diverge, and use the combined data to make decisions with both speed and confidence.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Human Participants Need

Human participants need nothing except a browser and a willingness to think aloud. There's no app to install, no Uxia account required, and no technical setup. When they open the link, they'll be prompted to grant recording permissions and then guided through the test.

## What Gets Captured from Human Participants

Every human session captures the following automatically:

| Data Type | Details |
| --- | --- |
| **Screen recording** | Full capture of everything on screen during the session |
| **Microphone audio** | Think-aloud narration throughout the test |
| **Face recording** | Optional — participants can enable their webcam for facial expression capture |
| **Click and interaction heatmaps** | Aggregated map of where participants clicked, tapped, and focused |
| **Transcripts** | Auto-generated transcript from the audio recording |
