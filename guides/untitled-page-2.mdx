## Setup

1. Go to the [Mobbin app page on ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com/apps/mobbin/asdk_app_69fdb9081018819193707354f21b366e).
2. Click **Connect**, which will bring up an "Add Mobbin to ChatGPT" dialog.
3. Click **Sign in with Mobbin** on that dialog.
4. Sign in with your Mobbin account to authorize access.

## Usage

Once connected, you can ask ChatGPT to search Mobbin directly:

```plaintext
Search Mobbin for onboarding screens from banking apps
```
