title: "Launch Your First Uxia AI Test in Under Five Minutes"
sidebarTitle: "Quickstart"
description: "Create your Uxia account, define a synthetic audience, launch your first AI test, and review actionable insights - all in under five minutes."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [https://platform.uxia.app/](https://platform.uxia.app/) and sign up / log in using your email address or a Google account.

    Once you're in, you'll land on the Uxia Home where you will see the Projects, Tests and Audiences you create.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a test type">
    Select the test type that matches your current goal. Each type is optimised for a different research goal:

    | Test type | Best for |
    | --- | --- |
    | [**AI User Test**](/testing/ai-user-test) | Validating designs, prototypes or URLs with task-based testing |
    | [**AI User Research**](/testing/ai-user-test) | Survey you audience to uncover needs and validate concepts |
    | [**Accessibility Test**](/testing/ai-user-test) | Validate the Accessibility of your designs using the WCAG 2.2 |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your test">
    Each test type has its own configuration options. Follow the guide for your selected test to learn how to set it up correctly before launching:

    - [**AI User Test**](/testing/ai-user-test)
    - [**AI User Research**](/testing/ai-user-test)
    - [**Accessibility Test**](/testing/ai-user-test)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define your audience">
    Tell Uxia who should test your product. Uxia uses this information to generate AI synthetic testers that represent your target audience.

    Use the Audience Builder to create your audience and select the participants who will take part in the test.

    Every audience is built using up to three layers:

    1. **Sociodemographic Filters** – Segment your audience using \+12 socio-demographic  filters, such as age, gender, education, income, occupation, and more.
    2. **Psychological Profiles** – Select participants based on personality traits and behavioral characteristics to better match your target users.
    3. **Audience Enrichment** _(Custom Plans only)_ – Enhance your audience with insights from your own research, customer data, or internal documentation to create more representative synthetic testers.

    **Shortcut:** Paste your product URL into the Audience Builder, and Uxia will automatically suggest an audience profile based on your product and industry. You can use the suggested audience as-is or customize it before saving.

    Once an audience is created, it can be re-used in any test.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch and wait">
    Review your configuration one final time, confirm everything looks right, and click **Launch Test**.

    Uxia's synthetic testers will begin their sessions immediately. You will see how the Test Transcripts tab keeps populating with the different interactions from your testers. Unlike traditional user research, there is no recruitment delay, no no-shows, and no scheduling coordination. Tests complete and return results within minutes.

    You'll receive a notification when your report is ready.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read your report">
    Open your completed test report to access three layers of findings:

    - **Think-aloud transcripts** — a verbatim record of each tester's narration as they moved through your product, including moments of confusion, delight, and hesitation
    - **UX scores** — SUS, SUPR-Q, and the Uxia Optimization Score, benchmarked against industry standards so you know where you stand
    - **Actionable insights** — auto-generated findings that summarise the key friction points, highlight what's working, and suggest specific areas to address

    Use the insights panel to prioritise fixes by impact, and share the report link with your team or clients directly from the dashboard.

    For **Human Tests**, Uxia also generates a shareable participant link that you can distribute to your own network or send to recruited participants — no additional tooling required.
  </Step>
</Steps>
