The **SSO and provisioning** tab is used for enterprise identity and access management features.

## What You Can Configure

Depending on your plan and enabled features, this tab may let you:

- Add and manage verified organization domains.
- Check whether a domain is configured, pending, missing, failed, or verified.
- Open the SSO admin panel.
- Open the provisioning panel for SCIM setup.
- Turn on or off **automatic account creation (JIT)**.
- Turn on or off whether invitations from external domains are allowed.
- Open audit logs setup.
- Open log streaming setup.

## Availability

<Note>
  If SSO or provisioning is not enabled for your organization, the page will prompt you to contact Uxia. These features are available on the Custom plan — [contact us](mailto:hello@uxia.app) to enable them.
</Note>

For more on how Uxia handles security and data, see the [Security page](/account/security).
