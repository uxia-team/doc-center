The **People** tab is where you manage access for users across the organization, including invitations, roles, and workspace membership.

## Available Actions

- View all users in the organization.
- See how many user seats are currently in use compared with your organization limit.
- Invite new users with the **Invite Users** button.
- Add one or multiple email addresses at once.
- Assign invited users to one or more workspaces during the invite process.
- Promote a member to **Owner**.
- Edit a person's workspace access.
- See users who do not yet belong to any workspace.

## Searching and Filtering

You can search and filter the people list by:

- User name or email
- Role
- Workspace

## Pending Invites

The **Pending invites** section lets you:

- View invites that have not yet been accepted.
- See which workspaces each invite includes.
- See who sent the invite.
- See when the invite expires.
- Resend an invite.
- Revoke an invite.

## Important Notes

<Warning>
  If your organization has reached its user limit, you will not be able to invite more users until the limit is increased. [Contact us](mailto:hello@uxia.app) to raise it.
</Warning>
