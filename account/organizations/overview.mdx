An **organization** is the top-level container for your Uxia account. It groups all of your workspaces, members, and plan limits in one place so organization owners can manage access, monitor usage, and configure enterprise identity features across the entire account.

## Accessing the Organization Page

1. Open the left sidebar.
2. Click your profile or avatar section at the bottom of the sidebar.
3. Select **Organization** from the menu.

<Note>
  If you don't see the **Organization** option, you likely don't have organization owner access. Contact your internal Uxia administrator to request it.
</Note>

## What You Can Do

The Organization page is divided into four tabs, each covering a different area of account management:

You can also use the **Contact us** link at the bottom of the Organization page if you need help with settings, permissions, billing, or account management.

## When to Contact Support

Use **Contact us** on the Organization page for help with:

- Organization permissions
- People access
- Workspace setup
- Billing or plan limits
- SSO, provisioning, audit logs, or log streaming
