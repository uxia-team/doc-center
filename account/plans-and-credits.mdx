## How Credits Work

Credits are the currency behind every test you run in Uxia. Each time you launch a test, credits are deducted from your balance based on the number of tester sessions in that run — one credit covers one tester session. Once a test is live and tester sessions begin, those credits are consumed.

Your credit balance is visible in your account dashboard at any time so you always know where you stand before you launch a new study.

<Note>
  The Free Trial is the best way to experience Uxia before committing. No credit card required to sign up.
</Note>

## Upgrading to a Custom Plan

When your research needs outgrow the Free Trial — more testers, more audiences, more test types, or enterprise controls — the Custom plan gives you unlimited capacity across the board. Custom plans also unlock Human Test recordings, Audience Enrichment with your own data, a branded workspace, priority support, and enterprise-grade security (SSO & SCIM).

A member of the team will walk you through pricing and help you find the right configuration for your organization.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens when my Free Trial credits run out?">
    Once your 50 Free Trial credits are used, you won't be able to launch new tests until you upgrade to a Custom plan. Your existing test results, transcripts, and reports remain accessible in your account — nothing is deleted. To continue running tests, [contact the Uxia team](mailto:hello@uxia.app) or book a demo at [uxia.app](https://uxia.app) to discuss a Custom plan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I test with more than 5 testers on the Free Trial?">
    No — the Free Trial caps each test at 5 tester sessions. This is enough to get meaningful signal from an AI-moderated study, but if your methodology requires larger panels or you want to run tests with no tester limit, you'll need to upgrade to the Custom plan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is the Custom plan available for agencies?">
    Yes. The Custom plan is well-suited for agencies and research teams managing multiple clients or product areas. With unlimited audiences, unlimited testers, and a custom workspace, you can keep each client's work organized and separate. Reach out to [hello@uxia.app](mailto:hello@uxia.app) to discuss a setup that fits your agency model.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get a demo or speak with someone at Uxia?">
    You can book a demo directly at [uxia.app](https://uxia.app) or send an email to [hello@uxia.app](mailto:hello@uxia.app). The team will get back to you to schedule a walkthrough of the platform and answer any questions about the Custom plan.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
