FAQs

## Got questions? We have answers

### What is AI User Research?

### AI User Research is a way to collect feedback from synthetic testers that represent your target audience. Instead of waiting for recruitment, you can ask targeted questions and receive qualitative insights in minutes. This type of testing consists on asking questions to our audience rather than asking them to navigate digital experiences. It is suitable for Research, Marketing, Design and Product teams.

### What are synthetic testers?

### Synthetic testers are AI-generated research participants designed to mimic specific user profiles, personas, or audience segments. In Uxia, they can be built to reflect your target audience and used to answer research questions, test ideas, or evaluate concepts.

### How is AI User Research different from AI User Testing?

### AI User Testing focuses on how users interact with a product, prototype, website, or flow. AI User Research focuses on asking questions to understand opinions, needs, motivations, expectations, and preferences. Uxia’s catalog positions User Research as interviewing AI testers from any target audience and getting answers in minutes.

### What can I use AI User Research for?

### You can use it to validate product ideas, test marketing messages, evaluate concepts, compare audience segments, uncover customer needs, and understand the “why” behind user behavior.

### Can I define the audience I want to research?

### Yes. You can create synthetic audiences based on personas, ICPs, segments, demographics, behaviors, and internal research. The more specific the audience, the more relevant the feedback. Uxia’s internal materials describe AI-generated synthetic testers that mimic specific target audiences

### Can I use it for concept testing?

### Yes. AI User Research can help you evaluate new concepts, product ideas, marketing messages, value propositions, and positioning before launch. It helps you see what resonates, what creates confusion, and what needs are still unmet.
