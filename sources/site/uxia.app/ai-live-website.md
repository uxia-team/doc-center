## Got questions? We have answers

### What is an AI Live Website Test?

### The AI Live Website Test lets you paste any URL - whether it’s a live website, prototype, or product - and instantly get feedback from AI testers that behave like real users. They explore your experience, identify friction points, and highlight what works and what needs improvement.

### How does AI user testing work?

### AI user testing works by giving synthetic testers a product experience and a specific mission to complete. The testers independently navigate the flow, interact with the interface, reason through each step, and identify points of confusion, friction, or unmet expectations. Uxia then turns those interactions into actionable UX insights, including summarized results, tester behavior, expectations, step-by-step reasoning, and usability scores.

### What kind of insights does Uxia provide?

### Uxia helps teams uncover UX friction, usability issues, user expectations, behavioral patterns, confusion points, and opportunities for product improvement. You can review summarized results for a quick overview, or go deeper into each tester’s step-by-step reasoning, interactions, expectations, and behavior across the flow.

### Do I need a fully built website to run the test?

### No. You can test anything with a public URL, including prototypes, staging environments, or partially built products. The AI testera adapt to whatever experience is available.

### Can I provide additional context or credentials to the testers?

### Yes. You can upload relevant files or provide credentials so the AI testers can access gated areas of your product. This allows for a more realistic experience and deeper insights, especially for logged in flows.

### Is my website data secure?

### Yes. Uxia processes your URL securely and does not store sensitive data beyond what’s needed to run the test and generate insights.

### Can the AI testers navigate any website?

### Not always. While the AI can explore most websites, some experiences may be partially or fully inaccessible due to technical limitations. If the site you want to test is not accessible by our testers, please reach out and we will check what are the alternatives.
