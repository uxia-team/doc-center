FAQs

## Got questions? We have answers

### Does Uxia provide the human testers?

### Currently, teams share the test link with their own users, customers, panels, or recruited participants. Uxia is designed to collect and analyze the feedback inside the platform.

### What do participants need to do?

### Participants open the link, review the mission and scenario, agree to recording permissions, and complete the test while their experience is captured.

### Can participants record their screen and voice?

### Yes. Human Insights supports screen recording and microphone. It can optionally capture face recording, depending on the study setup.

### Can I complement synthetic testers results with human insights?

### Yes. Human Insights is designed to complement synthetic testing by using the same mission, scenario, and prototype context.
