FAQs

## Got questions? We have answers

### What accessibility standards does Uxia use?

### Uxia checks designs against WCAG 2.2 criteria and supports different compliance levels, including AA and AAA.

### Can Uxia test Figma designs?

### Yes. Uxia can evaluate uploaded design screens. When a live URL is available, additional checks related to implementation can also be reviewed.

### Does this replace a full legal accessibility audit?

### No. Uxia helps teams identify and resolve accessibility issues earlier in the product process. For legal certification or formal compliance reviews, teams may still need specialist validation.

### Who should use the report?

### Designers, developers, product managers, researchers, and accessibility stakeholders can all use the report. Uxia separates issues by review scope to make ownership clearer.

### Why are some criteria marked as “review”?

### Some accessibility criteria require implementation details, human judgment, or code-level verification. Uxia flags those items so your team knows what needs additional review.
