Connect many identity providers to Uxia to enable secure SSO, automated user provisioning, and centralized access management across your organization.

## What you get

- **Single Sign-On (SSO)** — Let your team sign in with your existing identity provider, including Google Workspace, Okta, Microsoft Entra ID, and other SAML/OIDC providers.
- **Automated user provisioning** — Use SCIM and JIT provisioning to create, update, and deactivate Uxia accounts automatically as people join, move, or leave.
- **Centralized access management** — Control who has access to which workspaces from a single place, with role mapping from your IdP.

## Get started

Enterprise Auth is available on the Custom enterprise plan. See [Security & SSO](/account/security-and-sso) for setup details, supported providers, and security posture.
