Link Notion and Uxia to centralize documentation, project information, and team knowledge in one place.

## What you can do

- **Embed Uxia insights in Notion** — Bring transcripts, scores, and report summaries into your existing Notion workspace.
- **Centralize team knowledge** — Keep research briefs, test plans, and findings alongside the rest of your product documentation.
- **Share with stakeholders** — Give non-Uxia users a familiar place to read insights and follow along.
