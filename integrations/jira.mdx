Integrate Jira with Uxia to automatically sync tasks, issues, and project updates so everyone stays informed.

## What you can do

- **Push insights to Jira** — Turn findings from Uxia test reports into Jira issues without copy-pasting.
- **Keep status in sync** — Track which UX issues are in progress, resolved, or blocked directly from your Uxia workspace.
- **Align research and delivery** — Give product, design, and engineering a shared source of truth for what to fix next.
